# T4-rocky-karmakar-automation
QA assignment of Pravaah Consulting Inc
The following steps that we performed:
First I login to the application with the valid email id and password.
Then I click on 'Add new generator button' and give the generator name and internal account number.
After that I fill up the billing information by entering the industry, street address, email, phone.
I generate the latitude, longitude and verify those latitude and longitude.
Then I copy the billing information.
Then  give the service hours and copy the service hours to all days.
Then we click to 'Route assignment'. But before going to Route Assignment we have to go back to the generator information and make as the generator information as 'contracted'. Again go to the Route Assignment page .
Then I  click on 'Add Service button'. Give route, Service Type, Frequency ,Select weekdays. In Scope of Work I select 2 container, Select Anchor Date. Increase quantity for both container
Then i click on 'Add service to Route button' and successfully created the route.
After that I click on 'Price Book'. Here in 'Price Book' I toggle the view mode and update the service prices for both containers .
At last I logout the application successfully.
